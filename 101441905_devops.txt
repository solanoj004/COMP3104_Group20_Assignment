Welcome to the course!
In this course, we will explore the DevOps best practices along with some tools and technologies required to implement DevOps. 
By the end of the course, you will be prepared to explore job opportunities as a DevOps engineer. 
You will learn the procedures, protocols, and best practices that a DevOps engineer brings to a workplace environment to increase productivity. 
Analyze and evaluate various developer operation implementations that are available and implement hands-on work using the industry-standard toolkit. 
Create a solution to complex business problems by adhering to the core responsibilities of DevOps.
